# Musify Personalized product doctrine

Musify Personalized is a private, local-first, single-user music system built exclusively for Daniel. It prioritizes personal relevance, complete user control, exceptional reliability, intelligent automation, transparent recommendations, data ownership, and premium visual design over mass-market compatibility.

## Core rules

- Treat a song as a canonical musical identity, not merely a YouTube video ID.
- Prefer verified individual-song sources and reject compilations, long-form videos, podcasts, and unrelated uploads from normal music surfaces.
- Keep Favorites simple and immediate while allowing deeper optional taste feedback.
- Make smart playlists genuinely context-aware through audio characteristics and behavior rather than genre labels alone.
- Make the DJ intentional: every queued song and transition should have a reason, even when that reason is hidden from the user.
- Keep advanced controls optional so ordinary playback remains simple.
- Label source quality honestly. Never call transcoded or lossy audio lossless.
- Preserve user data through stable migrations, backups, resumable jobs, and source repair.
- Let album art and music create the visual environment; do not force Aquaz3y client branding onto Daniel's private app.
- Prefer original studio recordings, explicit versions when enabled, and the highest-quality reliable source.

## Major long-term systems

- Canonical library and multi-source playback
- Personal taste and behavior model
- Context-sensitive DJ and smart queues
- Late Night, Gym, and Driving smart playlists
- Intelligent AutoMix and transition learning
- Local and personal-server lossless playback
- Music Atlas: extensive sourced song, album, session, studio, instrument, personnel, cultural, and technical context
- Offline operation and Android Auto
- Cinematic, colorful, album-driven interface
